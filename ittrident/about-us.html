Our Process

Requirement Specifications

We will collaborate with you to pinpoint the exact requirements of the project. This will help us understand the specifications, what you plan to achieve with the software and how you want the solution to work.

Solution Design

We will begin designing the solution according to your requirements. A proto-type along with mock-up screens and interfaces will be provided, so that you can provide feedback. We will also conduct a demonstration to explain how the user will access.

Development & Testing

Once you are satisfied with the design, our team of developers will start coding your solution. After development, the solution will be tested so that any existing bugs can be rectified.

Deployment
& Maintenance

After the solution has been repeatedly tested and checked for errors, we can finally deploy it by integrating it into your business operations.
